Berhrends Travel Adventures

Paris July 19, 1955

Paris July 19th,1955- Paris July 19th,1955-

Took a motor from travel agency- after breakfest in our fancy round sitting room. Drove all around Paris the drive named Harvey has a daughter named at Waycross Georgia. Went to Lubin's for perfrume (had the pink flannel faffair that Harriet had given me- REsented ') W alked around the circle-Place de van6ome To Caron, The Ritz Hotel ,and a jewellers where I was intergues by a jewlled bird. But did NOT get it! To a very nice resturant in the Bois for lunch Le Pre Catalan. Went thento both sides of the Siene and got some little prints of Paris. R.R. station and Tourist Agency|reg. tickets. We had our evening meal at the Bristol and then set out for the Casion deParis. Very amusing show- we stayed thro half of it but felt we needed sleep. Gail got a cab by her shrill whistle.
